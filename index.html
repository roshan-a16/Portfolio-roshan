About Roshan A

I'm an aspiring MERN Stack Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Creative Graphic Designer passionate about building meaningful digital products that merge technology with thoughtful design. I enjoy transforming complex ideas into intuitive interfaces, scalable systems, and visually compelling experiences. From developing AI-powered solutions like Herb Scan an Ayurvedic Plant Classifier with a 90% accuracy rate using Django REST, MobileNet, and Render, to crafting usability-driven prototypes such as Gradify and Farm to Market, I focus on creating solutions that improve clarity, efficiency, and user satisfaction. As a designer, I've delivered logo and branding systems that increased client engagement by up to 60%, showcasing my ability to blend creativity with brand strategy.

Leveraging skills in MERN, design, and branding, I build experiences that fuse functionality with creativity to deliver real impact.

Services

I offer full-stack MERN development, UI/UX design, branding, and creative visuals,Delivering clean, functional, and aesthetic digital solutions.

MERN Stack Web Development

I build full-stack web applications using MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js. From dynamic frontend interfaces to scalable backend systems, I create fast, responsive, and user-centric web solutions.

UI/UX Design

I design intuitive and aesthetically pleasing user experiences backed by research, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes. My focus is on clarity, usability, and delivering interfaces that users instantly understand.

Prototyping & Product Design

Using tools like Figma and Adobe XD, I create interactive prototypes that bring ideas to life. This includes rapid ideation, user flows, interaction design, and validating concepts before development.

Graphic Design

I craft visually compelling graphics—from posters to digital creatives—that communicate brand messaging with clarity and style. My designs focus on strong layouts, typography, and visual storytelling.

Branding & Logo Identity

I develop complete brand identity systems including logos, color palettes, typography, brand kits, and mockups. My branding work helps individuals and businesses create a strong, memorable visual presence.

Motion Graphics & Cinematics

I create cinematic logo animations, promo videos, and short motion graphic sequences using After Effects. These add energy, professionalism, and personality to digital content.
